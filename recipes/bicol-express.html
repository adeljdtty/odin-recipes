Bicol Express

Description

Bicol Express is a spicy Filipino dish that originated in the Bicol region of the Philippines.

The dish got its name from the Bicol Express train, which used to run through the region.

Ingredients

  • pork belly
  • shrimp paste
  • coconut milk
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • onion
  • chili peppers

Steps

This are the steps in making Bicol Express

  1. Slice the pork into small pieces then saut√© it with garlic, ginger and onions.
  2. Add the shrimp paste to the mixture and pour the coconut milk and let it simmer until the pork is tender.
  3. Add the chili peppers, and put salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Cook for a few more minutes, then plate and serve.
