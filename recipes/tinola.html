Tinola

Description

Tinola is a traditional Filipino soup dish that is typically made with chicken, green papaya, and chili leaves.

It is a light, savory soup that is often served with rice and is popular in many regions of the Philippines.

Ingredients

  • chicken
  • garlic
  • onion
  • ginger
  • green papaya
  • chili leaves
  • water or chicken broth

Steps

This are the steps in making Tinola.

  1. Heat oil in a pot over medium heat. Sauté the ginger, onion, and garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add the chicken pieces and sauté for 2-3 minutes or until lightly browned.
  3. Pour in the water or chicken broth and bring to a boil. Skim off any impurities that rise to the surface.
  4. Lower the heat and let the soup simmer for 10-15 minutes or until the chicken is cooked.
  5. Add the green papaya and cook for 5-7 minutes or until tender.
  6. Season the soup with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Add the chili leaves and let it cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  8. Serve hot with steamed rice on the side.
