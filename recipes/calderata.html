Caldereta

Description

Caldereta is a traditional Spanish dish that has also been adopted and adapted in many Latin American and Filipino cuisines.

It is a hearty stew made with tender meat, usually beef or goat, that is simmered for hours in a rich tomato-based sauce with a variety of aromatic spices.

Ingredients

  • beef
  • tomato
  • garlic
  • onion
  • bell peppers
  • carrots
  • potatoes
  • paprika
  • red chili flakes

Steps

This are the steps in making Caldereta.

  1. Cook the beef on a large pan until browned on all sides.
  2. Remove the beef from the pan and set aside.
  3. In the same pot, add the onions and garlic and sauté until the onions are translucent and the garlic is fragrant.
  4. Add the sliced bell peppers and sauté for another 2-3 minutes until they are slightly softened.
  5. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook for 5-7 minutes until the mixture has thickened and the tomatoes have softened.
  6. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and simmer for about 1 hour or until the beef is tender.
  7. Add the cubed potatoes and sliced carrots, to the pan, then stir to combine and continue to simmer for another 30-40 minutes or until the vegetables are cooked through and the sauce has thickened.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste, then serve.
